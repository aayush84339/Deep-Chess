# ♟️ Deep Chess

Build a chess engine from scratch using **classical AI search** and **reinforcement learning**. You will implement ideas like **Minimax**, **Alpha-Beta pruning**, board evaluation, transposition tables and self-play.

By the end, your engine should be able to play complete games and make intelligent moves.

---

## 🧠 What You’ll Learn

- Board representation and legal move generation
- Minimax, Alpha-Beta pruning and evaluation heuristics
- Zobrist hashing and transposition tables
- Basics of NNUE-style evaluation
- Reinforcement learning with self-play and policy tuning
- Engine testing and tournament-style benchmarking

---

## 📅 Timeline

1. **Weeks 1-2:** Board representation, legal moves and game rules  
2. **Weeks 3-4:** Minimax, Alpha-Beta pruning and evaluation heuristics  
3. **Weeks 5-6:** Hashing, transposition tables and NNUE  
4. **Weeks 7-8:** Self-play, RL tuning, testing and final tournament  

---

## ⏳ Commitment

Expect around **5-6 hours per week**.

## 🏁 Final Showdown

At the end, all engines compete in a tournament.

May the best engine win.
